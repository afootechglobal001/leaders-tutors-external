# Curriculum Templates

This document provides curriculum templates for various external examination courses.

## IGCSE Mathematics Curriculum Template

### Course Duration
9 months (September - May)

### Topics Covered

#### Term 1 (September - December)
1. **Number**
   - Types of number
   - Calculation
   - Indices and standard form
   - Ratio, proportion and rate

2. **Algebra and Graphs**
   - Expressions and formulae
   - Equations and inequalities
   - Sequences, functions and graphs
   - Linear programming

#### Term 2 (January - March)
3. **Coordinate Geometry**
   - Straight-line graphs
   - Coordinate geometry problems

4. **Geometry**
   - Angles and properties
   - Triangles and constructions
   - Circle geometry
   - Mensuration

#### Term 3 (April - May)
5. **Mensuration**
   - Area, volume and surface area
   - Scale drawings and maps

6. **Trigonometry**
   - Right-angled triangles
   - Sine, cosine rules
   - 3D problems

7. **Vectors and Transformations**
   - Vectors in 2D
   - Transformations

8. **Probability and Statistics**
   - Data handling
   - Probability
   - Statistical diagrams

### Assessment Schedule
- Monthly progress tests
- Mid-term examination (December)
- Mock examination (March)
- Final revision (April-May)

2. **Kinematics**
3. **Dynamics**
4. **Forces**

#### Term 2
5. **Work, Energy and Power**
6. **Momentum**
7. **Matter and Materials**
8. **Electric Current**

#### Term 3
9. **D.C. Circuits**
10. **Practical Skills Assessment**

### A2 Level (Year 2)

#### Term 1

12. **Gravitational Fields**
13. **Ideal Gases**
14. **Temperature**

#### Term 2
15. **Thermal Properties of Materials**
16. **Oscillations**
17. **Electric Fields**
18. **Capacitance**

#### Term 3
19. **Magnetic Fields**
20. **Electromagnetic Induction**
21. **Quantum Physics**
22. **Nuclear Physics**

### Laboratory Work
- 16 practical sessions per year
- Continuous practical assessment

## SAT Preparation Curriculum Template

### Course Duration
12 weeks (Intensive preparation)

### Week 1-4: Foundations
**Mathematics**
- Algebra fundamentals
- Problem-solving strategies
- Calculator vs non-calculator sections

**Evidence-Based Reading and Writing**
- Reading comprehension strategies
- Grammar and usage rules
- Writing conventions

### Week 5-8: Advanced Topics
**Mathematics**
- Advanced algebra
- Geometry and trigonometry
- Data analysis and statistics

**Evidence-Based Reading and Writing**
- Advanced reading passages
- Command of evidence
- Analysis in science and history

### Week 9-12: Test Strategies & Practice
- Full-length practice tests
- Time management techniques
- Score review and improvement strategies
- Test-day preparation

### Practice Test Schedule
- Week 2: Diagnostic test
- Week 6: Mid-course practice test
- Week 10: Full practice test
- Week 12: Final practice test

## IELTS Academic Preparation Curriculum Template

### Course Duration
4 weeks (Crash course)

### Week 1: Listening and Reading
**Listening**
- Section 1-4 strategies
- Note-taking techniques
- Common traps and mistakes

**Reading**
- Skimming and scanning
- Question types analysis
- Time management

### Week 2: Writing Task 1
- Academic writing style
- Describing graphs, charts, diagrams
- Key vocabulary and phrases
- Practice and feedback

### Week 3: Writing Task 2
- Essay structure
- Argument development
- Coherence and cohesion
- Practice essays with feedback

### Week 4: Speaking and Mock Tests
**Speaking**
- Part 1: Introduction
- Part 2: Long turn
- Part 3: Discussion
- Pronunciation and fluency

**Mock Tests**
- Full-length practice tests
- Individual speaking sessions
- Detailed feedback

### Daily Schedule
- 09:00-11:00: Skills session
- 11:00-13:00: Practice and feedback

## General Teaching Methodology

### Assessment Strategy
1. **Initial Assessment**: Determine starting level
2. **Continuous Assessment**: Regular progress checks
3. **Mock Examinations**: Simulate exam conditions
4. **Final Review**: Address weak areas before exam

### Resources
- Official textbooks and study guides
- Past examination papers
- Online learning platforms
- Practice question banks
- Video tutorials and demonstrations

### Student Support
- Regular progress reports to parents
- One-on-one consultation sessions
- Extra materials for struggling students
- Advanced materials for high achievers

### Exam Registration Support
- Guidance on exam board registration
- Assistance with application procedures
- Exam fee information
- Important dates reminders
